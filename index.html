About Us

Established in 2007 by the visionary founder, Mr. Shareef bin Muhammed. Based in UAE, the Group has played an intrinsic role in the development of the country’s progressive economy and continues to harness its assets to deliver on a clearly defined mission. As a leading sustainable group of companies growing parallel with the country’s advancement, AL MAZAIA Group believes in creating prosperity for future generations across multiple industries.

Our Mission

Use our pioneering spirit and talented experts to constantly deliver sustainable growth through superior customer service, quality and commitment.

Our Vision

Our vision is to remain a leading sustainable group of companies providing prosperity for future generations across multiple industries.
